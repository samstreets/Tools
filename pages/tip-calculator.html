Tipping Guide (UK)

Restaurants

10–15% is standard in the UK. Many restaurants include a service charge — check before adding extra. Some restaurants keep service charges rather than passing them to staff, so ask if you want to tip directly.

Taxis

Rounding up or adding 10–15% is customary. For Uber and similar apps, in-app tipping is increasingly popular.

Services

10% is typical for hairdressers and similar personal services. Delivery drivers — a few pounds is appreciated but not obligatory.
