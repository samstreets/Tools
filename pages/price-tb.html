How It Works

Why Calculate Price per TB?

Comparing storage drives can be tricky when they come in different sizes and prices. This calculator helps determine cost-effectiveness by calculating the price per terabyte, making it easy to compare drives side-by-side.

How to Use

Simply enter the total price of the drive and its storage capacity in terabytes. The calculator will instantly show you the cost per TB and a value rating to help make informed purchasing decisions.

Pro Tip

Generally, larger drives offer better value per TB. However, consider your actual storage needs — buying more capacity than you need might not be economical if you won't use it within the drive's lifespan.

What to Look For

A lower price per TB indicates better value. Also consider drive speed (RPM for HDDs, read/write speeds for SSDs), warranty period, and brand reliability when making your final decision.
