02

Tips

Reverse Percentage

To find the original value before a percentage increase, divide the new amount by (1 + rate). E.g., if £120 is after a 20% increase, the original is £120 ÷ 1.20 = £100.

Commutativity Trick

8% of 25 is the same as 25% of 8 = 2. Use whichever calculation is easier in your head.

VAT Tip

To find the pre-VAT price from a VAT-inclusive price (20% UK VAT), divide by 1.20. To add 20% VAT to a net price, multiply by 1.20.
